PROJECT ABSTRACT

Brandwave is an advertising app that helps small and new companies reach potential customers without extensively advertising to those not interested hence saving resources that could be reinvested into the company to promote its growth.

Research has it that many new are not very not very knowledgeable about the kind or methods of advertising to reach their customers or target audiences. Due to the lack of effective advertisement many businesses collapse or declare bankruptcy or even fail to make enough sales to facilitate their operations. This kind of poor advertising happens because of the following reasons;

  • Improper use of passive media
  • reating ads instead of campaigns
  • Over targeting
To avoid these mistakes made by the business owners, the solution below has been made to provide necessary information to equip with techniques to carryout effective advertising and also to provide a medium for the adverts.
